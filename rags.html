RAGS TO EDUCATION (RTE) Movement

NOTE:
1) To know more please join us on 21st on September at 1300 hours or 1 PM at the Conference Hall, Admin. Block.

DESCRIPTION:
A Newspaper is a serial publication containing news. A Newspaper is usually but not exclusively printed on relatively inexpensive, low grade paper such as news print.
Total number of registered newspapersare 1,05,443 according to data released by the Registrar of Newspapers for India (RNI).
Majority of people avail the service of newspapers. Old newspapers which are usually thrown by people can be used to create source of education for those children who does not get education because of poverty.

We started this movement 3 months ago. We collected old newspapers from houses, shops etc. After collecting newspapers for 5 days we then sold them &the money we got after selling was used to open free tuition center for students who were unable to pay fee & also provided them books, notebooks & other stationary. Till Today 3 center’s are opened by this movement & 120 students are studying in these center’s. We give opportunity to unemployed youth for teaching in these tuition center& give them salary from this movement. We would like to exploit this unique moment &rise this social event Rags To Education (RTE).

Aim of this moment is:-
To provide education to each & every child of this country.
To provide books & other stationary.
To pay fee for needy students
To give them monthly scholarship
To give jobs to unemployed youth

OBJECTIVE: The Objective of this event is to motivate students to join us in this social moment.
